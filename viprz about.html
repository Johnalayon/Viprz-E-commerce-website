HISTORY

Our story began with a group of passionate gamers who saw a need for a gaming store that catered to the diverse needs and interests of the gaming community. We wanted to create a space where gamers could find the latest gaming consoles, cutting-edge accessories, and a wide selection of games, all under one roof.

In the early days, Viperz Gaming started as a small brick-and-mortar store in a local gaming hub, where we quickly gained popularity among gamers seeking quality products and exceptional service. Our commitment to delivering a personalized experience and understanding the unique requirements of each gamer set us apart.

As the gaming industry rapidly evolved, we recognized the importance of embracing online platforms. In 2017, we expanded our operations to include an online store, enabling us to reach gamers across the country and provide them with the same level of service and quality products they had come to expect from us.

With our online presence, we were able to curate an extensive catalog of gaming products from leading brands, including gaming consoles, gaming PCs, virtual reality systems, gaming peripherals, and a vast library of video games across various platforms. Our goal is to ensure that every gamer, regardless of their preferences, finds something that enhances their gaming experience and helps them achieve their gaming goals.

At Viperz Gaming, we understand that gaming is not just a hobby; it's a passion that brings people together. That's why we focus on creating a community around our store. We regularly organize gaming tournaments, events, and meet-ups, where gamers can connect, compete, and share their love for gaming. Our dedicated staff consists of avid gamers who are always ready to offer expert advice, recommendations, and technical support to help our customers make informed decisions.
